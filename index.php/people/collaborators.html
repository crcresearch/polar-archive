With a background in electrical-computer engineering, her research focuses on adapting tech for the sciences.  This includes utilising systems accross the spectrum from embedded systems and senors such as drones and IoT networks, through to HighPerformance Computing hardware and BigData software.  Through the whole stack she is particularly interested in wrestling with making the data as FAIR as possible and the tools as open as possible.
